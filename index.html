From Roots to Refreshment, Our Beverage Journey

What began as a humble dream in our parents' small kitchen has grown into a vibrant legacy built on taste, tradition, & innovation.

Years ago, our parents, driven by a passion for authentic flavors & natural ingredients, started a small-scale beverage business. With simple tools, time-honored recipes, & a deep love for Indian taste traditions, they handcrafted syrups & concentrates that quickly became local favorites.

Their signature blends, especially the zesty Nimbu Ginger, tangy Lemon Masala, & tropical Pink Peru, captured the hearts of families looking for refreshing & nostalgic flavors.

Now, as the next generation—a brother-sister duo with unwavering dedication, they were joined by a few close, precious friends as extended family—who believed in the vision. Together, they poured passion, trust, & innovation into every bottle, giving birth to a renewed brand with a modern heartbeat & traditional soul.—we are proud to carry the torch forward. Inspired by our parents’ dedication & guided by modern innovation, we are taking the brand to new heights.

We've introduced a line of Ready-to-Serve (RTS) beverages crafted from the same treasured recipes, perfect for today’s fast-paced lifestyle without compromising on purity or taste. Alongside, our range of concentrates continues to empower homes, cafes, & businesses to serve quality drinks with ease & authenticity.
